Owner of Tesla Inc.




Job Summary:
As the Owner of Tesla Inc., you will be responsible for leading the vision, strategy, and operations of one of the world's most innovative and influential companies. You will work with a talented team of executives and employees to drive growth, innovation, and sustainability in the automotive and energy industries.
Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop and communicate a clear vision and strategy for Tesla that aligns with the company's mission to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy.
  • Lead and inspire a team of executives and employees to achieve ambitious goals and deliver on Tesla's promise of innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction..
  • Drive growth and profitability by developing and executing strategic plans for expanding Tesla's product offerings, distribution channels, and global reach.
