McDonald's Manager



Job Summary:
As a Manager at McDonald's, you will be responsible for overseeing the daily operations of the restaurant, ensuring that all customers are served high-quality food and beverages, and managing a team of employees to achieve company goals.
Key Responsibilities:

  • Manage and train a team of employees, including hiring, scheduling, and providing ongoing feedback and coaching to ensure that they perform their duties effectively
  • Ensure that the restaurant meets or exceeds all customer service, food quality, and cleanliness standards established by McDonald's.
